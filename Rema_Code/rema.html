Preschool education is the provision of education for children before the commencement of primary education,usually at around the ages of 2 or 3 to 5 or 6. Preschool is also known as nursery school or kindergarten (other than in the USA and Canada, where kindergarten is the first year of primary education). Early learning, by comparison, covers all education of children from birth to the end of the preschool period, and includes learning at home as well as externally provided education. Wikiversity provides equally for all ages and stages of education. Toddlers, young children, and their parents and educators are welcome to create and share learning materials using this, the Wikimedia Foundation's website for global learning.
